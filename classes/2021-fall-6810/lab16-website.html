MIT gives you webspace and a (not-super nice but working) domain name.
For instance, for me it looks like: http://web.mit.edu/mueller_/www/6810/index2.html.

Setup How-To for Windows
  • download SecureCRT/FX
    • protocol: SCP
    • host: ftp.dialup.mit.edu
    • username or login name: Your Kerberos username (your e-mail address, without the @MIT.EDU part)
  • Go to www/
  • Create directory, name it 6.810
  • Go into 6.810/, upload your website files
  • View your website: http://web.mit.edu/YOUR KERBEROS/www/6.810/index2.html
  • Example page from Stefanie here: http://web.mit.edu/mueller_/www/6810/index2.html

Setup How-To for Mac
  • Download Fetch
    • Host: ftp.dialup.mit.edu
    • Username or login name: Your Kerberos username (your e-mail address, without the @MIT.EDU part)
    • Connect using: SFTP
  • Use the "Get" button to retrieve files, "Put" to upload files.
  • Go to www/
  • Create directory, name it 6.810
  • Go into 6.810/, upload your website files
  • View your website: http://web.mit.edu/YOUR KERBEROS/www/6.810/index2.html
  • Example page from Stefanie here: http://web.mit.edu/mueller_/www/6810/index2.html
