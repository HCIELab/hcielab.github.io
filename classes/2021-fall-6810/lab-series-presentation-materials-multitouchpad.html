6.810 Engineering Interactive Technologies (fall 2021)

Lab Series: Presentation Materials Multi-Touch Pad

In this lab series, you will create presentation materials for your multi-touch pad.

Your presentation materials consist of four parts. First, you will create a rotoscope by drawing on top of a photo. Next, you will take photos with differently colored backdrops. After this, you will create a short video of your multi-touch pad. Finally, you will add the different presentation materials to a website.



This lab series consists of the following labs:
  • Lab 13: Drawing Rotoscopes (1.25%)
  • Lab 14: Taking Photos & Photo Editing (1.25%)
  • Lab 15: Video Recording & Editing (1.25%)
