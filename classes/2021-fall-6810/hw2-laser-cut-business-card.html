6.810 Engineering Interactive Technologies (fall 2021)

Design and Laser Cut Your Business Card

In this lab, you will design and then fabricate your own business card with a laser cutter.

Steps:

  1. Create Business Card Design
  2. Choose Material
  3. Laser Cut Business Card

Deliverables

At the end of the lab, upload to your student google drive:
  1. laser cut file for your business card (.svg or .pdf file format)
  2. at least one photo of your business card (make a photo with clean background, i.e. we don't want to see crap in the background, put your card on a white piece of paper or hold it in front of a white wall to take the photo)
  3. bring your card to the next class

(1) Create Business Card Design

Google for 'business card' for some inspiration.

Once you have your 2D design ready, please upload it to your student google drive folder as .svg or .pdf. Let a TA know on slack you have your design ready, we will check if all the settings (line color, thickness) are correct (see tutorials under 'useful resources' above). Once the TA confirmed your design is ready, you can pick one of the timeslots in an OH to come in and laser cut your card (note that the earliest OH will be tuesday since first week is remote).

Please check out:
  • How to create a drawing for Laser Cutting

(2) Choose Material

We will have a range of different acrylics and wood ready for you to choose from.
Below you see a selection of the materials we bought.
They include fluorescent, translucent, transparent, and opaque acrylic in a bunch of different colors.
We also have wood 3mm, which can look very nice when engraved.
