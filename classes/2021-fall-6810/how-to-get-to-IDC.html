IDC (N52-337): How to get there


IDC Campus Location


The IDC (Building: N52) is located next to the current location of the MIT Museum.


IDC Door Access


Pay close attention which door you are using. Look at the campus map above and below. The big black dots indicate which doors are working. All other doors will not unlock when you use your card. The door you need to use is NOT the door that is right next to the MIT Museum. Instead, you have to walk around the corner, there is a ramp there for accessibility access right next to a parking lot. If you go up the ramp, this is where the working door is.





Take Elevator to 3rd Floor (N52-337)


After entering the building, go straight ahead, then take it right.
You should see an elevator. Take it to the third floor (International Design Center.)



Go to IDC Workshop Space


When you exit the elevator on the third floor, take a left turn.
Go straight until the end of the hallway.
Then turn right, use your card with the card reader to access the IDC space.
Keep going straight until you reach the IDC workshop on the left side.
