Homework: Design and Laser Cut Your Business Card


Deadline:

Friday, September 11, 2020, 10pm

Deliverables:

Upload the following to your google drive:
  • at least one photo of your business card (make a photo with clean background, i.e. we don't want to see crap in the background, put your card on a white piece of paper or hold it in front of a white wall to take the photo)
  • laser cut file for your business card (.svg or .pdf file format)
  • bring your card to the next virtual class

Task:

The title says it all.
Design and laser cut your own business card, upload the deliverables to your google drive.
We will have a range of different acrylics and wood ready for you to choose from.
Google for 'business card' for some inspiration.

Useful Resources:

Please check out:
  • How to create a drawing for Laser Cutting
  • How to operate the laser cutter

What to do at home and when to come in for laser cutting:

You create the 2D design at home.
Once you have your 2D design ready, please upload it to your student google drive folder as .svg or .pdf.
Let a TA know on slack you have your design ready, we will check if all the settings (line color, thickness) are correct (see tutorials under 'useful resources' above).
Once the TA confirmed your design is ready, you can pick one of the timeslots in an OH to come in and laser cut your card.
