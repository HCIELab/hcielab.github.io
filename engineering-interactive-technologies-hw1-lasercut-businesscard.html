Task:
 The title says it all. Laser cut your own business card, upload the deliverables to gradebook, and bring your card to class. Materials, design etc. is all up to you. We recommend using paper, cardboard, acrylic, or wood. Google for some inspiration.
Where can I laser cut?
  • Cypress Engineering Design Studio (EDS)

    • shop managers: David L Lewis and Gavin Darcey
  • International Design Center (IDC)

    • shop manager: Chris Haynes and Oksana Lasowsky
  • use Mobius App to find a makerspace on campus
  • or try a local Fablab, let us know how it goes!
Where can I buy materials?
  • Altec Plastics has all sorts of acrylic (15 min on the redline)
  • paper / cardboard from the local craft store
  • Chris Haynes in IDC has some wood panels you can use, ask him
